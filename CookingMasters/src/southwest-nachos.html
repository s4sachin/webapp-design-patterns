Southwest Nachos

12 ingredients | 30 minutes | Appetizer

Description

A delicious and colorful appetizer of nachos loaded with Southwest-inspired flavors, including black beans, corn, tomatoes, and avocado.

Ingredients

Tortilla chips
1 large bag
Black beans
1 can (15 ounces), drained and rinsed
Frozen corn
1 cup, thawed
Cherry tomatoes
1 cup, halved
Jalapenos
1-2, sliced
Red onion
1/4 cup, diced
Cheddar cheese
2 cups, shredded
Monterey Jack cheese
2 cups, shredded
Avocado
1, diced
Fresh cilantro
1/4 cup, chopped
Sour cream
for serving
Salsa
for serving

This is a project for a Frontend Masters workshop by Maximiliano Firtman.
